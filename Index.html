About us

The Visionary

90 Voices Mass Choir project is a musical group that was
formed by the award winning Cape Town based gospel artist
Chumani Ngojo. He formed the group to win more souls for Christ.
This is a group where young people are given a chance to showcase
their talents be it music,dance or art.
This group has more than 200 members.
This project took off 2 years ago in Khayelitsha.
Chumani held auditions for people who think they have the
required talent to join the group and with the help of some
of the Cape Town artists and Pastors this project
became a success.
